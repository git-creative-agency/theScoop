ASU Retirees Association Re-entry Scholarship
The ASU Retirees scholarship was first established in 1995 and is intended to benefit a student returning to college after being in the work force.
Learn more and apply online.
Arizona Community Foundation Scholarships
The Arizona Community Foundation allows high school seniors and current college students throughout Arizona to apply for more than 90 scholarship awards online with one general application.
Learn more and apply online.
Pathways for the Future Scholarship
The Pathways for the Future Scholarship for degree seeking students was created to provide learners the knowledge necessary for life and work in the 21st century.
Learn more and apply online.
Gary Hilliard FQC Scholarship
The Gary Hilliard FQC Scholarship awards $2500 a full-time sophomore, junior, or senior enrolled in a two- or four-year institution. The Flexographic Technical Association Foundation and the Flexo Quality Consortium sponsor this scholarship.
Learn more and apply online.
FFTA Scholarship
The FFTA Scholarship awards $3000 to multiple students interested in the fields of flexography and/or graphic communication. The Flexographic Technical Association Foundation sponsors this scholarship.
Learn more and apply online.
Graduate College University Grant
The Graduate College University Grant (GCUG) is a need-based award to support new and continuing masters and doctoral students with financial need as determined by information taken from the FAFSA. Students must be nominated by their academic unit.
Learn more online.
Ira A. Fulton Schools of Engineering Scholarships
These scholarships are available to both new and continuing engineering students. The application process is open annually in early November, and most scholarships have a deadline of February 1.
Learn more and apply online.
Adobe Design Circle Scholarships
Adobe believes that young people should have the opportunity to learn and express themselves through creativity and technology, regardless of their economic or cultural backgrounds. Adobe will be awarding 10 annual college scholarships to help reach untapped youth communities and foster more diversity in the design industry.
Learn more and apply online.
AIGA Worldstudio Scholarships - Closes April 7
AIGA Worldstudio Scholarships benefit underrepresented and economically disadvantaged students who are studying photography, illustration, and design disciplines.
Learn more and apply online.
