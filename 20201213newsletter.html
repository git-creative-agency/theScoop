Don't miss Innovation Quarter's free events over your break!
View this email in your browser
Innovation Quarter lasts through Jan. 8 and will immerse attendees in fun, intriguing topics and creative projects that explore new ideas, build connections and develop valuable professional development skills.

Sessions range from academic themes like getting into medical or law school, to professional and career development themes, to lighter, more social themes like crafting with the dean, movie and book clubs, and more. All sessions are free to attend!
Find an upcoming session on the Innovation @ ASU website.
