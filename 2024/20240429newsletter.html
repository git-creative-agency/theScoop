Jobs and Internships
Student Creative Lead Tempe Campus
University College seeks a designer who looks beyond graphics production, who takes creative ownership of a body of work, communicates with stakeholders, leads other designers, and translates the vision of the project into a final product.
Apply through ASU. Requisition ID: 100886BR.
Student Software Developer Tempe Campus
The School of Sustainability seeks a student to contribute to the development of a multi-player game. This is part of a research grant of the National Science Foundation to study how people make decisions in uncertain environments. The developed game, Port of Mars, is used for research.
Apply through ASU. Requisition ID: 100825BR.
Student Graphic Design Assistant Tempe Campus
The ASU Center for the Study of Race and Democracy seeks a student to design for print, digital, and social media.
Apply through ASU. Requisition ID: 100737BR.
Student Digital Content Assistant Downtown Phoenix Campus
Arizona PBS seeks a student to assist in the production of digital assets for Arizona PBS programs and series. This position will focus on video editing, to include re-purposing existing material for digital platforms as well as assisting in the production and editing of new content.
Apply through ASU. Requisition ID: 100524BR.
Student Content Marketing and Events Assistant Tempe Campus
The School of International Letters and Cultures seeks a student to create marketing materials, provide event support, help to maintain their website, develop and send emails using Salesforce, and to develop and implement both online and print marketing communications materials.
Apply through ASU. Requisition ID: 100632BR.
Student Experiential Learning Initiatives Visual Designer Tempe Campus
University College seeks a student to complete graphic design projects in print and web formats to be used in online webpages, email and newsletter distribution, promotional giveaways, social media marketing, video production, data visualization, and slide decks.
Apply through ASU. Requisition ID: 99971BR.
Full-Time - Quantitative UX Researcher Multiple Locations
Meta seeks an experienced individual contributor who will help elevate the practice of research with creative but rigorous qualitative, quantitative, and/or design research methods. The right candidate will be an effective communicator, passionate about understanding people, curious about the relationship between data, technology and society, and a great collaborator.
Learn more and apply through the Meta website.
