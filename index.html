Read Past Issues of The Scoop

Established by the student-led GIT Creative Agency in 2017, The Scoop is sent to current and past students of the program, students at ASU who have enrolled in GIT courses, faculty, staff, and industry members. A source for finding jobs, events, announcements, and many other helpful reminders, The Scoop is written and coded by students, for students.

Please note that for newsletters created before May of 2019, images may be missing.
