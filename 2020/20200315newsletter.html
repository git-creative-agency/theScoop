Important information about ASU courses starting this week.
View this email in your browser
ASU students who attend classes in-person on campus should have already seen communications from ASU and their professors about what to expect upon their return from spring break. As of Monday, March 16, professors have been asked to offer their in-person classes remotely to help prevent the spread of the COVID-19 virus. Online courses will continue as usual.
ASU has many resources available for students, and instructions and help can be found on the provost website.
