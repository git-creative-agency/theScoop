Announcements
MIX Center Worlds for Change Weekend
Worlds for Change Weekend is a 2-day hackathon inviting ASU undergraduate students of all backgrounds to help prototype an upcoming worldbuilding contest kicking off later this year for ASU's newly launched XRts graduate degrees. Open to current ASU undergraduate students, this is a chance to learn worldbuilding skills and immediately put them into action prototyping a creative vision for the future. No experience required. Win fun prizes, including scholarships!
Learn more and register online.
SIGGRAPH SpaceTime 2023: Call for Student Submissions
Help us celebrate the 50th anniversary of SIGGRAPH.
This graphic design competition provides an excellent opportunity for students to exhibit their computer-based media creative work nationally and internationally. Entries will be judged by international jurors based on the solution of the theme, artistic merit, design, originality, technical excellence, and content.
Learn more and enter on the SIGGRAPH website.
Scholarships
PRINTING United Alliance Scholarships - Closes April 30
Supported by generous donations from endowment investors and industry leaders, the 2023 PRINTING United Alliance Scholarship Program will award $2,000 to 25 students studying graphic communications, granting a total of $50,000 in educational assistance for the 2023-2024 academic year.
Learn more and apply online.
PGSF Scholarships - Closes May 1
The Print and Graphics Scholarship Foundation has coordinated the printing industry's largest scholarship program since 1956. Individuals, companies, and associations provide both annual and endowed scholarship funds to PGSF, which administers the scholarship and fellowship programs.
Learn more and apply online.
PGSF Grant Program
The Print and Graphics Scholarship Foundation (PGSF) grant program was developed to fund activities and equipment for high school, trade school, community college, and university graphic communications programs. Applications may be submitted for travel expenses or equipment.
Learn more and apply online.
Adobe Scholarships & Fellowships
